Public and Products Liability (including Care, Custody and Control coverage) - Policy placed via CGU Insurance

  • Cover for our Space Traders against any legal liability arising from the following services: Packing, pick up, delivery, unloading and/or moving items of storage as per Spacelli’s Terms and Conditions
  • $10,000,000 limit any once occurrence for Public and Products Liability (legal liability 
  • $100,000 care, custody and control of third party property (cover for any negligent care, custody and control of Space Seeker goods and items by the covered Space Trader)
  • $250,000 for errors and omissions (incidental advice and professional services in relation to the covered activities i.e. Alleged misleading and deceptive conduct by the covered Space Trader)
  • Main Exclusions:
    • Damage caused by the interruption or cutting off of amenities such as Gas, Water and Electricity to the Space Traders space
    • Policy excludes property damage whilst the stored goods and items are static (refer Storage Insurance Policy with CGU for this risk)
    • Hazardous Goods and Items i.e. Any forms of dry or wet chemicals
    • Flammable Goods i.e Any goods that have a high flammable rating
    • Perishable Goods i.e. Any livestock, fish, marine life, plants, vegetation, soils and other organic  substances
    • There is no cover for first party personal injury
